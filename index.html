🏆 Todo Priority Bracket

Rank your to-do list by playing a tournament — pick winners head-to-head, get a ranked list.

Paste your todos, then choose the more important of two at a time. Every item is seeded into a single-elimination bracket; even the items that lose early get fully ranked through consolation rounds, so you end with a clean #1-to-#N priority order.

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.
